Notes
  • Script kiddies, identity thieves, social engineers, phishers get their definitions in the MCQs.
  • Some other attackers are industrial spies, criminal groups, hackers, botnet operators. Victims cannot be labelled so easily. Anybody can fall victim, when the attack is not targeted. This can happen especially in social media, and one of the main attack-facilitating features there is forwarding or sharing.
  • The MCQ on user-forwarding entities may be tricky, but instructive at the same time. Note that a troll is a person, ransomware is a program. A meme virus is not a computer virus. A Nigerian letter may not come from Nigeria. Don't try to memorize the answer number, but find out what risks all these entities involve, and how they spread.
