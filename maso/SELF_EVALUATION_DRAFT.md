C1: I understand information, computing, networking and cyber systems as platforms where threats can target data, users, devices, software, networks and services.

C2: I see cyber security as related to information security, but with special emphasis on networked systems, attackers, digital services and wider social effects. I no longer think of it only as confidentiality.
